Adventure Travel

Scuba Diving

Scuba diving is Thailand’s most popular water sport. One of the top 10 adventurous things to do in Thailand, any trip remains incomplete without having a glimpse of the underwater world. With 2000 km of coastline, hundreds of islands, there are reefs, walls, caves, wrecks to be explored.

Paragliding

A perfect adventure sports for the adrenaline-junkies! Tandem paragliding has become the centre-stage sports in the regions of Oludeniz Beach in Turkey. This sleepy town off the Mediterranean coast, south of Turkey is home for the famed Blue Lagoon bedrock for paragliding.

White Water River Rafting

White-water River rafting is one of the leading adventurous things to do in Thailand.The rivers generally have fast flow, rapids of grade I- V, and rocks and other obstacles. As the raft makes its way on the river you feel the adrenalin rising.

Rock climbing

Rock climbing is one of the adventurous activities for those who love challenge, excitement and outdoor sports. Although it is considered as a dangerous activity, rock climbing is increasingly popular among adventure enthusiasts. Thailand has the best spots for rock climbing in the world.
